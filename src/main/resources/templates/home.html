Behind the scenes: The story behind NLTI

Here's why NLTI is indispensable for your CLAT Preparation!
Run by NLSIU Students Exclusively

NLTI is an initiative started by NLSIU Students who have cracked CLAT with flying colours.

Unprecedented Selection Ratio

NLTI has produced over 100 NLS, NALSAR and NLUD Selections and more than 500 selections across NLUs.

Personalised Mentorship by NLSIU Mentors

Get 24/7 access to NLSIU Students who will act as your best friend, guide, and mentor.

Daily DCS and Weekly Live Doubt Clearing Sessions

All your doubts will be solved in real-time via Telegram Chat and live concept/strategy sessions will be taken weekly.

Study Buddy Groups

You will be put in a group of like-minded study buddies to ensure competitiveness and resource sharing among aspirants.

Comprehensive Test Series

You will be provided 50 CLAT, 10 AILET, 10 OLET and 100 Sectional Mocks in our course coupled with personalised time tables and schedules.

Revolutionizing Success: Our Results Speak Louder Than Words

NLTI takes immense pride in the extraordinary achievements of its students, which stand as a testament to the revolutionary approach we embrace. Our results speak volumes about the exceptional quality of mentorship and study material we provide, setting new benchmarks in the realm of CLAT and NLSAT success.

Our previous batch saw more than 15 NLSIU selections, 25 NLU-Delhi selections and over 200 selections across NLUs. NLTI broke all records in AILET 2024 when our students clinched 6 out of the Top-10 ranks, showcasing not just their academic prowess but also the effectiveness of our holistic approach towards education.

Join NLTI and experience the difference that quality mentorship and material can make in realizing your dreams!
